The SDLC AI-Impact Analyzer is an interactive data-driven application that quantifies how AI tools are transforming each phase of the software development lifecycle. It aggregates 210+ real-world data points from academic research, industry surveys, vendor reports, and practitioner anecdotes spanning 2023–2026.

The application covers six SDLC phases: Strategy, Design, Spec, Dev, QA, and DevOps. Each phase is analyzed with statistical measures (median, quartiles, min/max) based on the underlying evidence.

Key capabilities include: interactive dashboards with real-time filtering, trend analysis comparing early AI era vs. agentic AI era, an ROI calculator for building business cases, and a comprehensive source library with full traceability.
